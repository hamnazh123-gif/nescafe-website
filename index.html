Brewing Ritual

The Perfect Golden Cup

Make coffee brewing an intentional ritual. Follow these steps to unlock the full potential of NESCAFÉ Gold.

01

The Measure

Add 1 to 2 teaspoons (approx. 2g per cup) of NESCAFÉ Gold to your favorite mug, depending on your preferred strength.

02

The Temperature

Pour hot (but not boiling) water (approx. 85°C / 185°F). Water that is too hot can burn the delicate micro-grains and ruin the aroma.

03

The Infusion

Stir gently. Watch the golden crema bloom on the surface, releasing the rich, locked-in coffee oils. Allow to cool slightly and savor.
